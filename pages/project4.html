Parking lot simulation

My team focused in: Improving agent decision-making (cars) to be able to find your assigned place inside of a parking lot, in turn respecting the agents traffic lights as well as junctions without a traffic light. Making a simulation in unity combined with mesa. The environment is a parking, which has routes through where the cars can circulate, each one with its own sense, there are traffic lights at a couple of crossings, a roundabout where cars can go anyway the car decide, parking drawers where cars can to be stored and drawers with carts already stored as well as buildings in the middle of the parking spaces.
