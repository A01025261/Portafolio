Device Interconnection

Communication between computing devices is a feature that is likely to be taken for granted nowadays. Being able to count on a reliable computer network, stable and secure, it is a basic need of any organization. So with the detailed analysis of the information collected and knowing the capacities and specific needs, my team designed a network that complies in conditions of structuring, continuity, wiring and interoperability, protocols in IP networks and optimization with continuous monitoring.
