Projects

Here are the projects that I consider have been the most important so far in my focus stage, which cover the 4th and 5th semester of my degree.

Watch the Tempo!

The objective of this project was to arouse the interest of young people, children and adults in percussion instruments through an interactive, entertaining and didactic video game. And it is hoped that by being their first approach to the subject they will become familiar with the risks involved in long practice sessions, all thanks to the mechanics included in our serious game.

Device Interconnection

For this project we were asked to design a network at various levels (logical, physical, devices, configuration) that would serve for a competition called International Collegiate Programming Contest that will be based at a university in Mexico in this case the "Instituto Tecnológico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey (Campus Estado de México)"

Notificando Atizapán

This application and web page were developed with the main porpuse of helping all the inhabitants of the municipality of Atizapán, with personalized civil protection alerts, which will alert the citizens of Atizapán about weather reports, floods, fires, closed streets and the seismic alarm replication.All of it in order to prevent a most serious accident before it occurs and Civil Protection and the nearest firefighters can carry out their work more uickly and efficienttly, in addition to taht will allow citizends to be better prepared and informed

Parking lot simulation

Nuestro reto consiste en proponer una solución al problema de movilidad urbana en México, claro que tomando en cuenta un punto de vista centrado en evitar la congestión vehicular utilizando una simulación visual para representarlo, siendo este una salida de un sistema multiagente. Y nos enfocamos en: Mejorar la toma de decisiones de los agentes (automóviles) para poder encontrar su lugar asignado dentro de un estacionamiento, a su vez respetando los agentes semáforos así como los cruces sin semáforo.
